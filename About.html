About KRS

Our Story

KRS Talanta Football Academy is dedicated to nurturing young football talent in Tanzania. Our mission is to provide high-quality training, education, and opportunities for aspiring footballers to reach their full potential both on and off the field.

Founded in 2018, our academy has a rich history of developing skilled players who have gone on to achieve success at various levels of the sport. We believe in fostering a positive and inclusive environment where players can grow, learn, and thrive.

Our vision is to become a leading football academy in Africa, known for excellence in training, player development, and community engagement.
